Things To Do

Walking around downtown Winter Park is an activity in and of itself between all of the restaurants and shops. Below are some of our favorite activities and food spots around the area.

Restaurants

Briarpatch

Our favorite brunch spot in Central Florida. There's always a line but it goes quick and the food is worth the wait.

Fun Fact:
Anneliese's Hinge profile picture was at this restaurant. So one could say... our relationship started here.

www.thebriarpatchrestaurant.com
252 N Park Ave #3814
Winter Park, FL 32789
(407) 628-8651

Reel Fish

Fresh, affordable, delicious seafood with a great happy hour menu.

Fun Fact:
This was the first place we went after we chose our wedding venue.

www.reelfishcoastal.com
1234 N. Orange Ave
Winter Park, FL 32789
(407) 543-3474

Prato

One of our favorite date night spots. Fresh authentic Italian food with pasta and pizza that will literally melt in your mouth.

Theme Parks

Walt Disney World

Perfect spot for families and friends of all ages! Has thrill rides, kids rides, top-tier entertainment, and amazing food options.

Magic Kingdom - The most iconic of all Disney World parks, home to Cinderella's Castle, and best for families with small children. Classic rides here include Space Mountain, Big Thunder Mountain, Pirates of the Caribbean, and The Haunted Mansion to name a few.

Epcot - Perfect for adults who are looking for a more relaxing stroll through the World Showcase tasting food and drinks from around the globe. Our favorite rollercoaster, Cosmic Rewind, is here as well.

Hollywood Studios - Ideal for Star Wars and Toy Story fans. Also home to classic rides like Rockin' Rollercoaster and Tower of Terror.

Animal Kingdom - The most relaxing Disney park. Has the perfect combination of a zoo and theme park with the realistic Kilamanjaro Safari and the best live show on property, The Festival of the Lion King.

www.disneyworld.disney.go.com
1180 Seven Seas Drive Lake Buena Vista
Orlando, FL 32830
(407) 939-5277

Universal Studios

Universal Studios Orlando houses the Wizarding World of Harry Potter across both theme parks! Grab some delicious butterbeer and spend a day exploring one of Central Florida's premiere attractions.

Universal Studios - Ideal for Star Wars and Toy Story fans. Also home to classic rides like Rockin' Rollercoaster and Tower of Terror.

Islands of Adventure - Great theme park for thrill seekers with plenty of high speed roller coasters like The Incredible Hulk, Velocicoaster, and Hagrid's Motrobike with plenty more to choose from.

www.universalorlando.com
6000 Universal Blvd
Orlando, FL 32819
(407) 363-8000

Sea World

The perfect pairing for marine lovers and thrill seekers. Some of the fastest and largest roller coasters in Central Florida like Manta, Kraken, and Pipeline: The Surf Coaster can be found here with plenty of activities for the smaller children as well. Take in a live animal show, learn more about sea life, and enjoy some great food along the way.

www.seaworld.com
7007 Seaworld Dr
Orlando, FL 32821
(407) 545-5550

Outdoors

Take a guided kayaking tour through Winter Park's scenic canals. Great for wildlife spotting and a nice workout all wrapped in one.

Orlando, FL 32789
(407) 212-7306

Other Activities

Winter Park Village

Outdoor shopping plaza walking distance from Springhill Suites and just a few minute drive from The Alfond Inn. Great food options here include Hangry Bison, Bar Italia, Sakari Sushi, and Jeni's Ice Cream. You can also catch a movie at the Regal movie theater or relax with a massage at the Hand and Stone spa.

www.shopwinterparkvillage.com
510 Orlando Avenue
Winter Park, FL 32789
(407) 571-2700

Winter Park Farmers’ Market

Weekly Farmers' Market in the heart of Winter Park. Open Saturdays from 8 a.m. - 1 p.m.

www.cityofwinterpark.org
150 N New York Ave
Winter Park, FL 32789
(407) 599-3341

Orlando Science Center

Orlando Science Center is fun for the curious minds of all ages. This interactive learning experience includes dinosaurs, solar systems, agriculture, STEAM, and so much more! Also offers an amazing rooftop view of the city.

www.osc.org
777 E Princeton St
Orlando, FL 32803
(407) 514-2000
